Mebe MemeCoin, the Ultimate OTC DEX for trading Airdrop Allocations, Points, and Tokens, has launched the xWHALES Airdrop, a dynamic incentive program designed to reward users for their trading activities on its platform, complemented by a robust Referral Program.

About Mebe MemeCoin

Mebe MemeCoin revolutionizes peer-to-peer cryptocurrency trading with its innovative trustless decentralized OTC platform, providing a secure and transparent environment for traders across various blockchains to facilitate the seamless exchange of Airdrop Farmingallocations, Points, Tokens, NFT Whitelists and Runes, through pre-markets.

The $WHALEStoken is the backbone of Mebe MemeCoin, driving platform growth transparently through fee-sharing mechanisms and buyback features. Additionally, users can leverage LootBot's innovative on-chain terminal, for streamlined Airdrop tracking across multiple chains.
